**EARTH‑I™** is the master ecosystem that coordinates the sovereign technology stack for environmental governance, industrial compliance, atmospheric verification, and biophysical intelligence. EARTH‑I™ consists of four primary system layers:

* **CONSTELLATION OS™** — The master coordination layer responsible for synchronizing all operating systems, governing release cycles, and maintaining cross‑repository alignment.
* **ATMOSPHERIC OS™** — The active atmospheric verification engine that tracks methane, carbon dioxide, and decadal drift using satellite‑grade data pipelines.
* **BIOSPHERE OS™** — The biophysical intelligence layer that monitors soil biology, canopy health, hydrology, and ecological asset stability *(Fully scaffolded and pending public release)*.
* **INDUSTRIAL OS™** — The sovereign industrial telemetry and compliance engine that monitors energy, water, carbon, labor integrity, and ecological impact across industrial domains.

##  Core Principles

EARTH‑I™ operates on strict operational constraints to maintain systemic integrity:
* **Sovereignty First:** All data remains strictly within designated jurisdictional boundaries and cannot be externally harvested, extracted, or inferred.
* **Deterministic Accounting:** Cryptographically sealed ledger entries rooted in Natural Law alignment and regeneration metrics over extraction cycles.
* **The 28/3 Cadence Protocol:** Grounded edge-nodes run on fixed 28-day autonomous operational cadences with dedicated 3-day synchronization and verification loops.
* **Barefoot Integrity:** Strict verification protocols generating legally defensible audit trails accompanied by absolute zero-trace hardware deletion systems (**Burn-On-Sync™**).
